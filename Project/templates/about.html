About Our Project

Our movie recommendation web app is designed to provide users with personalized movie suggestions based on their preferences. Users can discover movies by either filling out a form, answering a set of questions, or simply providing a text prompt describing their mood, genre preferences, or specific interests. The app utilizes an intelligent recommendation system that analyzes user input and suggests movies that best match their tastes, ensuring a seamless and engaging experience for cinephiles.

Developed by a team of passionate innovators : Shreekar, Tavishi Srivastava, Dhruv Mishra, Ekta Saini, Ripu Dhaman Singh, and Sanyam Kabra. The project aims to enhance movie discovery through an intuitive and user-friendly interface. Whether users seek classic hits, trending blockbusters, or hidden gems, the app curates recommendations tailored to individual preferences, making movie selection effortless and enjoyable.

We hope you enjoy using our movie recommendation web app and discover new films :)
